Privacy Policy for Bounny

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Introduction

Bounny ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Audio Data

Bounny uses your device's microphone to record audio for speech recognition and transcription purposes. Audio recording only occurs when you actively initiate it within the app. We do not record audio in the background or without your knowledge.

  • Audio is processed to generate text transcriptions and summaries.
  • Audio data is transmitted to our servers for processing by AI language models.
  • Audio data is not stored permanently on our servers after processing is complete.

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Username
  • Usage data (token count, usage statistics)

Device Information

We may collect basic device information such as device type and operating system version for app compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide speech-to-text transcription and AI-powered summarization services.
  • To manage your account and track usage for billing purposes.
  • To improve and maintain the app's functionality.

Data Storage and Security

Your transcription records are stored locally on your device using an encrypted database. Account credentials are stored using Android's encrypted shared preferences. We use industry-standard security measures to protect data transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party AI language model providers (such as OpenAI) to process your audio transcriptions and generate summaries. These providers have their own privacy policies governing data handling.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time from within the app.
  • You can use the app as a temporary user without creating an account.
  • You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Bounny is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
